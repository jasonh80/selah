## The 9 that need revision (plain English)

1. **Jesus — the critical one.** The profile says "**reported** resurrection" — twice. Selah writes from conviction, not a secular-historian hedge. Fix: "Risen from the dead." One-word theology fix, non-negotiable before any use.

2. **Jacob** — his stage ages are wrong by ~35 years (the profile's own verse math puts him at ~77–97 in Haran, but it says 40–60). Fix the numbers or label a deliberately younger depiction.

3. **Moses (image profile)** — burning-bush scene assigned to "~40s" Moses, but he was ~80 there. A key chapter would render the wrong man. Split the life stage.

4. **Samson** — described as "powerfully built," which produces gym-superhero art and contradicts the story (nobody could tell where his strength came from). Fix: lean, wiry, unremarkable build with the uncut Nazirite hair.

5. **Isaiah** — contains a factual error (temple-measuring vision belongs to Ezekiel) and the highest risk in the cast of an image model painting God as a man in the Isaiah 6 throne scene. Delete the error; add the guardrail.

6. **Daniel** — same danger: Daniel 7's "Ancient of Days" could render as a literal old-man-God. Add: render the vision through beasts, thrones, and Daniel's terror — never God as a human figure.

7. **Aaron** — the high-priest garments are under-specified (gold plate needs the blue cord and early-Semitic engraving spelled out, or image models will render English lettering).

8. **Levitical Priest archetype** — actually describes the *High* Priest. Used for any temple crowd, every priest gets a jeweled breastpiece. Split into High Priest + ordinary priest (plain linen).

9. **Temple environment** — one identity block for two different temples (Solomon's vs Herod's). Split by era or renders will drift or wrongly match across a thousand years.
